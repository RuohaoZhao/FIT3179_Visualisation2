Global Covid19 Report in 2020

The first coronavirus was identified in Wuhan, China in December 2019. Immediately, the virus was infecting throughout China. As the virus spreads more and more severely, the whole world is suffering from the Coronavirus.

Total Cases and Deaths of Each Regions


Although the total number of cases in Asia in this pandemic was just less than that in North America. However, the Asian measures taken in time to control the spread of the epidemic resulted in a relatively low number of deaths compared to North America.
